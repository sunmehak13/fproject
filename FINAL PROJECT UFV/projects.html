Project for Cintas: Truck Tracking Software

Completed: April 2024

I developed a software solution for a Fortune 500 company Cintas (Langley Warehouse) to streamline their process of tracking trucks being loaded and unloaded. This project aimed to enhance efficiency and accuracy in their logistics operations, reducing manual errors and improving overall workflow. The software provided real-time updates on the status of each truck, ensuring better management and coordination within the logistics team.

RECKLESS: Luxury Clothing Brand Website

Completed: February 2024
